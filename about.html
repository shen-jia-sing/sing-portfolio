About Hi, I'm Sing

Playful in spirit, disciplined in execution.

Playful in personality and focused in execution, I thrive in collaborative and creative environments while maintaining a strong commitment to quality, responsibility, and results. Outside of my work, I enjoy staying active through sports and have a strong passion for learning constantly exploring new ideas, skills, and technologies to grow both personally and professionally.

How I Got Here

I am currently a fourth-year student at the American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP), majoring in Information and Communication Technology (ICT). My journey into technology started with a curiosity about how digital products are built, which naturally led me to explore both front-end and back-end development. Over time, this hands on experience shaped me into a fullstack developer who enjoys turning ideas into functional, user-friendly solutions. Beyond technology, volleyball has played a major role in shaping who I am today. As the current President of the AUPP Volleyball Club, I’ve had the opportunity to lead, motivate, and grow a competitive team. Under my leadership, our team achieved second and third place finishes across multiple tournaments and earned several medals. These experiences taught me discipline, teamwork, and leadership skills that strongly influence how I approach my work in tech. I’m deeply motivated by continuous learning and personal growth. I actively seek out new knowledge, whether through hands-on projects, certifications, or competitive environments. This combination of technical curiosity, leadership experience, and a strong drive to learn is what brought me to where I am today and it continues to push me forward.
